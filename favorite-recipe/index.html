Blueberry Muffins

A delicious blueberry muffin with a crumb topping. Use fresh blueberries for best results!

Ingredients

  • Muffin Ingredients
    • 2 cups blueberries
    • 1 1/2 cups white flour
    • 3/4 cups sugar
    • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
    • 1/2 tsp salt
    • 2 tsp baking powder
    • 1/3 cup vegetable oil
    • 1 large egg
    • 1/3 cup milk, or more as needed
  • Crumb Topping Ingredients
    • 1/2 cup sugar
    • 1 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
    • 1/2 cup white flour
    • 1/4 cup butter, cubed

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 400Â°F.
  2. Line muffin pans with wrapers for 12 regular muffins, or 6 jumbo muffins.
  3. Prepare the muffins:
    1. Whisk the flour, sugar, baking powder, cinnamon, and salt in a large bowl.
    2. Pour oil into a liquid measuring cup. Add the egg and enough milk to reach the 1-cup mark. Stir until combined. Pour into the flour mixture and mix until the batter is just combined. Batter should be very stiff.
    3. Fold in the blueberries gently with a rubber spatula, then set the batter aside to rest.
  4. Prepare the crumb topping: combine sugar, flour, butter, and cinnamon in a small bowl, and mix with a fork or pastry cutter until crumbly.
  5. Spoon the batter evenly into the muffin cups, then spoon the crumb topping evenly over all the muffins.
  6. Bake in the oven until a toothpick inserted in the center of a muffin comes out clean of batter, typically 22-25 minutes for regular-sized muffins.
