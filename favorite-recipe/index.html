Blueberry Muffins

A delicious blueberry muffin with a crumb topping. Use fresh blueberries for best results!

Ingredients

  • Muffin Ingredients
    • 2 cups blueberries
    • 1 1/2 cups white flour
    • 3/4 cups sugar
    • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
    • 1/2 tsp salt
    • 2 tsp baking powder
    • 1/3 cup vegetable oil
    • 1 large egg
    • 1/3 cup milk, or more as needed
  • Crumb Topping Ingredients
    • 1/2 cup sugar
    • 1 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
    • 1/2 cup white flour
    • 1/4 cup butter, cubed
